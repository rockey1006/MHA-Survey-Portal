# Feedback

Use this page to collect qualitative and quantitative feedback from advisors, students, and administrators using the Health platform.

## How to submit feedback

- Complete the Google Form linked below to share feature requests, bugs, or usability comments.
- Responses route to the product team’s shared spreadsheet for triaging during weekly stand-ups.

[Feedback Form](https://forms.gle/aemasLp9KTbpU5Jz8)

## Reviewing responses

- Feedback owners should review new submissions at least once per sprint.
- Categorize entries (bug, enhancement, usability, data request) and create corresponding tickets in the project backlog.
- Close the loop with the reporter when an issue is resolved or deployed.

## Additional channels

- Direct email: `health-product@tamu.edu`
- Slack: `#health-feedback` (if available within your workspace)
- Office hours: first Tuesday of each month, 2–3 PM CST (update as schedules change).
